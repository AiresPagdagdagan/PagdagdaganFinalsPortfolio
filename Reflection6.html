R6 - Color Theory and Typography

Color theory consists of the set of rules and guidelines designers use to interact with users by attracting visual interface color schemes. To always choose the right colors, designers use a color wheel and use detailed information on human optical, psychological, cultural and other subjects. Then besides that your colors have to represent the goal and personality of your design to maximize a positive psychological effect on the users. Typography is far more than the option of beautiful fonts: it is a critical component of the design of the user interface. Good graphics will have a high visual hierarchy the website will have a graphical balance and the overall tone of the product. Typography can direct, educate and optimize readability and usability and ensure excellent experience for your users.
