A4 - Invitation Design Activity

1.) How can we improve this? You can point out details and give comments for improvements.

Writing an invitation card is simple. Address your card to the person you’re inviting; name the event you’re inviting them to; spell out the date, time and location; and provide contact details so they can RSVP and also when creating an invitation card it must be direct to the point so that it can easily understand by the participants who joins to your party. VIU application is just like a Netflix but the difference is Netflix is universal that you can watched all your favorite videos but the VIU is all about Korean but I’m pretty sure my favorite application is the VIU application no application can replace it ..

Steps for improving your invitation card:

1.) 1st step to improve the invitation must be removed the word (cordially) and place the word (You are invited to the), so that it may looks formal and direct to the point to the participants.

2.) 2 nd step also removed the word (delectable) so that it may also looks formal and direct to the point when the participants read the invitation card.

3.) 3 rd step should be capitalized the first letter of the name of the sender (Robert and Alexandra).

4.) 4 th step be specific when giving a date so I decided to add it on (Saturday, 30th of June) so that the participants wont confused of when they will attend the party.

5.) 5 th step 9:30 in the evening should be (9:30 P.M) to be shortcut.

6.) 6 th step Change the word (nibble) to (Wine and Snacks). (Nibble it means takes small bites out of.) It means it is an action.

7.) 7 th step always put an (Address) inside the invitation card so that the participants know the place where they go.

8.) 8 th step Include an RSVP (for the response of participants who will join the party).

2.) What would be the objective function? Tell me what the passage is all about. What is the objective?

The used of the passage is to know the selected participants who are invited in the invitation, the passage will tells us that this is Robert and Alexandra’s after-dinner party and the wine and snacks will be present on the day of month of June 30th, 2012 at exact 9:30pm. The venue of the party will be launched at Robert and Alexandra’s residence so that the participants will direct to their venue.

The objective of this invitation is to reserve positions for the guests prior to the date the occasion is scheduled and knowingly all the participants who will be attending to the after-dinner party by replying or response or leaving a message, so that it make sure that it would be clear to the participants where they go and know what time they attend to be exact so that they won’t be late in the party
