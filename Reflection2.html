Design thinking processes its sound like creating an art from the scratch which the scratch turns into masterpiece because of the design thinking process being planning all the design that been applied now we create a good quality of products based on the good design thinking planned by the person. Also Design thinking I think it is very helpful to us a developer when it comes designing a project website, and other stuffs that need design thinking process.
