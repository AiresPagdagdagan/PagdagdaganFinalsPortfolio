What is Design Thinking?

Design thinking is a natural characteristic that person have and also it is a process for a practical and a creative resolution of problems or issues that looks for an improved future result. It is the essential ability to combine empathy, creativity and rationality to meet user needs and drive business success. Unlike analytical thinking, design thinking is a creative process based around the building up of ideas. Design thinking includes imagination and reason, a combination of convergent and divergent thought, and creativity.

And also design thinking allows solutions to be developed creatively and quickly, and tested so that it gives a good result or having a one of a kind masterpiece that never forget. Design thinking revolves around a deep interests or a developing an understanding of the people. Also design thinking it helps as observes and develops empathy. Also helps us to think quickly to solve the problem in the shortest period. Design thinking also consist of brainstorming sessions so that it helps to collect lots od idea and prototyping so that it helps also improved the product if there’s anything to improved so that it will finalized the products. Also design thinking involves ongoing experimentation like sketching, prototyping, testing and trying out concepts and ideas which it helps to improved and make the product outcome beautiful and finalized as a masterpiece

The importance of design thinking is to generate and explore new things that aims to solve a concrete for human need and it is important to us of having a design thinking so that we tackles all the problem that are difficult to define so that it can solve it easily because of the ideas collected and by that it helps to define ambiguous problems and in turn makes it easier to surface solutions,
