Reflection #5

R5: Visual Information Design

I learned that visual information use of one or more of the various visual media with or without sound. Generally, visual information includes still photography, motion picture photography, video or audio recording, graphic arts, visual aids, models, display, visual presentation services, and the support processes.

We talked about the last time the activity that was given to us and besides that to how we can improve the invitation properly by editing it and let us attach something to make it presentable. Like for example this kind of invitation “You are cordially invited to Robert and Alexandra’s delectable after-dinner party. Wine and nibbles will be served. when: June 30th, 2012 at 9:30pm. Where: the pad. If you need directions, ping us. Kindly let us know if you’ll be attending by June 1st” so that I used to deleted some words does not appropriate to the passage and change it into formal words that would be fit to the details inside the invitation for making the invitation more presentable and understandable.

I focuses on the design of the invitation too much so that the audience would attract and easy to understand what would be the intention of my invitation, I thought a simple design would be useful for making an invitation just like my classmates said do it minimalistic so that it would be easy to understand the invitation and easy to identify the details inside the invitations.
